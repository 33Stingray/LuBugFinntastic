Chain Reaction



  1. Think of the last thing that will happen. (example: hit a marble)

  2. Then, think of the thing before that, and keep doing that until
    you have the first thing that you want to happen.

  3. This won't take a long time if you think it will.
    After you are done thinking, start building it!

  4. Once you are finished, test it out to see if it works!
